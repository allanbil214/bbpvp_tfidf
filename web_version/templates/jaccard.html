About Jaccard Similarity

Formula:

J(A,B) = |A ∩ B| / |A ∪ B|

Where:

  • A ∩ B = Intersection (common terms)
  • A ∪ B = Union (all unique terms)
  • Result: 0 to 1 (0% to 100%)
