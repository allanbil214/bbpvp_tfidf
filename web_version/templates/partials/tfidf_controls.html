Formula Reference
Term Frequency (TF):
TF(t,d) = count(t,d) / |d|
Inverse Document Frequency (IDF):
IDF(t) = log(N / df(t))
TF-IDF:
TF-IDF = TF × IDF
Cosine Similarity:
cos(θ) = (A·B) / (||A|| × ||B||)
